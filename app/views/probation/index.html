As soon as possible after release

Register at a local GP

It usually takes 2 weeks to register at a GP, but this can take longer at some GPs. You will need to be registered to make an appointment or get medication.

  • Find a GP near me
Get proof of ID

You will need proof of ID to apply for benefits and to register at a GP.

  • How to get Proof of ID
  • What proof of ID do I need?
Apply for benefits

You are living in Sheffield, which means you can apply for Universal Credit. This replaces several benefits:

  • Child Tax Credit.
  • Housing Benefit.
  • Income Support.
  • income-based Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA)
  • income-related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Working Tax Credit.

You can get help applying for benefits at a local job centre.

  • How to apply for Universal credit
  • Find a job centre
