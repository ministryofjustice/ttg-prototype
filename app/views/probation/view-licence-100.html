Your licence conditions

Purpose of release on licence

  • To protect the public
  • To prevent re-offending
  • To promote successful reintegration into the community

Standard conditions

You must:

  • keep in touch with your supervising officer, as instructed by them
  • have visits from your supervising officer, as instructed by them
  • permanently live at an address approved by your supervising officer and get their permission to stay at a different address for 1 or more nights
  • only do work approved by your supervising officer (including volunteering) and notify them before any change
  • not travel outside the United Kingdom, the Channel Islands or the Isle of Man without getting permission from your supervising officer
  • be well behaved and not do anything which could undermine the purpose of your supervision (protect the public, prevent re-offending, promote successful reintegration into the community)
  • not commit any offence.

Extra conditions

You must:

  • stay at your place of curfew from 2100 each evening until 0800 each morning
