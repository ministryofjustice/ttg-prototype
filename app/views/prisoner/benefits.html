Benefits

If you're not working, its important to apply for benefits so that you have some money.

Universal credit

You're living in Sheffield which means you can apply for Universal credit. Universal Credit is a payment to help with your living costs.

It’s paid monthly. You can find out how uch you may get using a benefits calculator:

  • entitledto
  • Turn2us
  • Policy in Practice

You can also get an advance payment, which means you'll get some money straight away if your claim is accepted.
